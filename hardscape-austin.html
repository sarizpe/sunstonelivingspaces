Transform Your Outdoor Space with Custom Hardscaping

Sun Stone Living Spaces specializes in creating beautiful, functional outdoor living spaces through expert flagstone and brick work. From elegant patios and walkways to custom fire features and retaining walls, we bring craftsmanship and creativity to every hardscape project in the Austin area.

Serving homeowners throughout Austin, Round Rock, Cedar Park, Pflugerville, and surrounding communities, we understand how to work with Central Texas terrain and climate. Our hardscape installations are built to last, combining durability with timeless beauty that enhances your property's value and your outdoor living experience.

Our Hardscape Services Include:

  • Flagstone Patios: Custom-designed outdoor living spaces with natural stone
  • Brick Work: Traditional and modern brick installations for patios and walkways
  • Stone Pathways: Beautiful walkways connecting your outdoor spaces
  • Retaining Walls: Functional and decorative stone walls for terracing and landscaping
  • Fire Features: Custom brick fireplaces and fire pits for outdoor entertaining
  • Outdoor Kitchens: Stone and brick work for outdoor cooking areas
  • Driveways & Borders: Decorative stone edging and driveway enhancements
