## 🎯 Next Steps (In Order)

### 1. Update Your Contact Information (5 minutes)
Open each HTML file and replace:
- Phone: `(512) 555-1234` → Your actual phone
- Email: `info@sunstoneliving.com` → Your actual email
- Update service area if not Austin, TX

### 2. Add Your Project Photos (15 minutes)
- Take photos of your best work
- Resize to ~1200px wide
- Save to `images/gallery/` folder with these names:
  - lawn-1.jpg, lawn-2.jpg, lawn-3.jpg
  - hardscape-1.jpg, hardscape-2.jpg, hardscape-3.jpg
  - bathroom-1.jpg, bathroom-2.jpg, bathroom-3.jpg
  - kitchen-1.jpg, kitchen-2.jpg, kitchen-3.jpg

### 3. Add Social Media Links (2 minutes)
In each HTML file footer, replace `href="#"` with your actual URLs:
- Facebook: `https://facebook.com/yourpage`
- Instagram: `https://instagram.com/yourpage`
- LinkedIn: `https://linkedin.com/company/yourpage`

### 4. Test Your Website (10 minutes)
- Open `index.html` in your browser
- Click through all pages
- Test the contact form
- Check on your phone
- Have someone else review it

### 5. Publish Your Website (30 minutes)
Choose one option:

**Option A - Free & Easy (Recommended for Starting):**
1. Go to [Netlify.com](https://netlify.com)
2. Sign up for free
3. Drag and drop your `sunstone-website` folder
4. Get instant website!

**Option B - Traditional Hosting:**
1. Purchase hosting (Bluehost, HostGator, etc.)
2. Upload files via FTP
3. Point your domain

## 🎨 Customization Tips

**Change Colors:**
- Open `css/styles.css`
- Edit the `:root` section at the top
- Change color values to your preference

**Edit Text:**
- Open any `.html` file in a text editor
- Find the text you want to change
- Edit and save

**Add More Photos:**
- See detailed instructions in `README.md`
- Copy existing gallery items in `gallery.html`
